Welcome to the Myfare Animal Adoption and Rescue Services

Myfare Animal Adoption and Rescue Services is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing animals, providing them with quality care, and finding them loving forever homes.

About Us

We are a team of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about animal welfare and ensuring that every animal has a loving home.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and loving environment for animals in need, and to find them loving homes where they can thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every animal has a loving home and is treated with the care and respect they deserve.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We believe in treating every animal with kindness and empathy.
  • Integrity: We are committed to being honest and transparent in all our actions.
  • Collaboration: We work together with other organizations and individuals to achieve our mission.

Services offered

Animal Adoption

We provide a comprehensive animal adoption process to ensure that every animal finds a loving home. The adoption process includes a thorough screening of the possible adoption options to ensure the best match for the person and animal at large.

Rescue Services

We provide comprehensive rescue services to help animals in need. Our team of dedicated volunteers works tirelessly to ensure that every animal receives the care and attention they deserve.

Spay and Neuter Programs

We offer spay and neuter programs to help control the animal population and prevent unwanted litters. Our team of dedicated volunteers works tirelessly to ensure that every animal receives the care and attention they deserve.
